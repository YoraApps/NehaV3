About the Founder


Neha D Gupta   Founder, is an Internationally certified   corporate and soft skills trainer. She has initially received her training from the Image specialist Judith Rasband (CEO and Director of Conselle Institute of Image Management) who is amongst the First Image Specialists in the world. Later on she went on to pursueAdvanced Image Consultancy and Professional Development  from one of the world’s best institute “LONDON IMAGE INSTITUTE “.

Being a professional  Image consultant, Etiquette and Grooming Specialist she had worked with people of all walks of life right from building confidence in school students to creating a right attitude for teenagers to  indulging the right style to corporates and re building confidence in senior citizen . she has conducted and presented various workshops be it for the cooperate world or teens and adults., in India and abroa Since her early age, neha was always an admirer of personalities and etiquette, growing up to be the boss of her own she only realized her passion to change someone life and help them achieve success by instilling self love and confidence and hence PEGASIS was born. Neha has travelled around the globe to countries like France ,Italy that has infused her mind in inculcating various international styles that she imparts to her clients . She is also the co founder of CONFIDENT YOU that conducts workshops for teens and adults .She says she enjoys the most about her work is to see her client’s transformation and hear how they feel about themselves post session . she was the Image Consultant for the Movie “ Hai Apna Dil To Awara” .she has been featured on Bombay times and fox news and also interviews by www.bbcnews.com and radio – on the importance of Image Consultation and Image Building exercises as the secret of soaring popularity of many Bollywood stars who thrive in the showbiz industry even without having a drool-worthy physique
